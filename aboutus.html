"In a world where women are expected to be dependent on men,

we believe that we must celebrate our independence & strength

and be unapologetic about it,

because only then we can be seen and respected for the strong individuals that we are.""

 

VALUE 1

 

Born into this world, you are loved by the ones who patiently await your  arrival. 

 As the years go by, you learn how to give and receive love. 

Often times in life, circumstances change, and we often neglect our own needs and desires. 

We recognize how important it is to be selfish with the love you give.

 There is only one you. 

Embrace Her. 

Love Her.

 BE Her.

 

VALUE 2

 

A woman's strength is beautiful. Powerful. 

Unlike any force in this world. We create life in our wombs. Birth your children. 

WE are the pillars of society. 

It's time to recognize our power. Embrace our strength. 

Celebrate US. 

Today, tomorrow, forever.

 

VALUE 3

 

For centuries, women have been known to stay home: 

"cook, clean, and take care of their kin. The man will provide. "

We say: No More.

Strong, Independent women. That is who we are. 

 We go through life's challenges and come out stronger.

 We break societal norms and stand in our power. 

 We believe in Independence. 

 We believe in You.

 

VALUE 4

 

I AM WOMAN.

 I am Free. 

 Free from limitations.

 Free from expectations.

 I live life on my own terms.

 I am Confident, Strong, and Unafraid. 

 WE are WOMEN.

Fonts

The FemmeForward Heading fonts are strong, bold & luxurious looking fonts that represent what we stand for. The body font of Ubuntu is a bit softer with rounded edges, to emphasize the feminine target group of our brand.

Logo

Our logo is designed to express power, luxury and recognizability. With the combination of the font and the colors used in the logo, we try our best to already give the target audience a certain first impression about our brand and what we stand for.

Colors

Our color palette is designed to express luxury, power and has a nice mix of bold, popping gold and brown with the softer white pastel. Our color palette allows for an immediate indication as for what kind of brand we are and what we represent.
