“In a world where women are expected to be dependent on men,
we believe that we must celebrate our independence and strength
and be unapologetic about it,
because only then can we be seen and respected
for the strong individuals that we are.”

-Femme Forward

Self-Love

Born into this world, you are loved by the ones who patiently await your arrival.
As the years go by, you learn how to give and receive love.
Often times in life, circumstances change. We neglect our own needs and desires.
We recognize how important it is to be selfish with the love you give.
There is only ONE you.
Embrace Her.
Love Her.

BE Her.

Unapologetic

Being a woman means I am free.
I live by no one's rules but my own.
By being me, fully, I inspire others.
There is a certain power in embracing who you are and what you stand for.

I will not be silenced.

Independence

For centuries,
women have been known to stay home, cook, clean, and take care of their kin.
"The man will provide."
We say: No More.
Strong, Independent women. That is who we are.
We go through life's challenges and come out stronger.
We break societal norms and stand in our power.
We believe in Independence.
We believe in the Future.

We believe in You.

Strength

A woman's strength is beautiful.
Powerful.
Unlike any force in this world.
We create life in our wombs. We birth your children.
WE are the pillars of society.
It's time to recognize our power.
Embrace our strength.
Celebrate US.
Today, tomorrow,

Femme Forward.
